Welcome to my collection of learning resources. Here, I will continue to add materials primarily focused on Machine Learning and Statistical Methods. Over time, I may reorganize these topics in a more structured and interpretable way for easier navigation.

[Visualizing A Neural Machine Translation Model (Mechanics of Seq2seq Models With Attention) - Jay Alammar](https://jalammar.github.io/visualizing-neural-machine-translation-mechanics-of-seq2seq-models-with-attention/) - Another excellent illustration by Jay Alammar, focusing on the mechanics of attention in sequence-to-sequence models.

4. [Understanding LSTM Networks](https://colah.github.io/posts/2015-08-Understanding-LSTMs/) - A short, crisp description of LSTM networks for beginners.<br>
I recommend reviewing the basics of RNNs before diving into the LSTM stuff here. Although the article briefly introduces RNNs, it is still worthwhile to explore some core concepts, such as hidden states and time unrolling to build a stronger foundation.
